Kakapo Chalet

Nestled in the heart of the alpine wilderness, our Kakapo chalet is a haven of rustic luxury. A cozy fireplace and floor-to-ceiling windows reveal stunning mountain vistas. The gourmet kitchen and spacious deck invite shared moments, while bedrooms offer comfort and style. Recharge in the outdoor hot tub or unwind in the sauna. Ski-in/ski-out convenience ensures adventure at your doorstep, with charming villages nearby. Discover the perfect blend of mountain thrill and relaxation in our enchanting Kakapo chalet.

Kereru Chalet

Welcome to our exquisite Kereru chalet, where alpine beauty meets luxury living. With its rustic yet elegant design, this slopeside retreat offers panoramic mountain views through expansive windows.
